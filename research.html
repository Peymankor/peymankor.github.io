Research

My research is focused on the question of "What is the Optimal Time to Invest in New Technology", the study requires the formulation of a dynamic programming model. I deeply value interdisciplinary collaboration, and welcome you to contact me if you are interested. You can find detailed information about my publications, software, and other work on my CV.

Multi-Stage Learning and Decision Making
